Types of Automobile Insurance Coverage

There are various types of automobile insurance coverage options, including liability coverage, collision coverage, and comprehensive coverage. Liability coverage is required by law in most states and covers damages caused to other drivers in an accident. Collision coverage covers damages to your own vehicle, while comprehensive coverage covers damages to your car that are not the result of an accident, such as theft or natural disasters.

When using free automobile insurance quotes online, it is important to consider the different coverage options available and choose the policy that offers the best protection for your vehicle and driving habits.
