For anyone struggling to find motivation or joy this holiday season, I recommend finding a Merry Christmas Hope Quote that speaks to you.

The True Meaning of Christmas

It’s easy to get caught up in the commercialization of Christmas and forget about its true meaning. One of my favorite Merry Christmas Hope Quotes that remind me of the true meaning of Christmas is “For unto us, a child is born, unto us, a son is given, and the government will be upon his shoulders.
