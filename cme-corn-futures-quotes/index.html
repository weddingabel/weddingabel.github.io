How Cme Corn Futures Quotes work

Cme Corn Futures Quotes represent the market’s expectation of the future price of corn. Traders can buy or sell futures contracts based on these quotes, which ultimately represent an agreement to buy or sell corn at a certain price on a certain date in the future. If the market price of corn rises or falls before that date, the trader can either make a profit or a loss depending on the direction of the change.

For example, let’s say a trader buys a futures contract for corn at $4 per bushel with delivery in three months. If the market price of corn rises to $6 per bushel within those three months, the trader can sell the contract for a profit of $2 per bushel. However, if the market price of corn falls to $2 per bushel, the trader will experience a loss of $2 per bushel.
