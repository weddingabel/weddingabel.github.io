Question and Answer Section about Education Quotes By Indian Philosophers

1. Who was Rabindranath Tagore?

Rabindranath Tagore was a Bengali polymath and the first non-European recipient of the Nobel Prize in Literature. He was a philosopher, poet, musician, and artist, and his works continue to inspire us to this day.

2. What did Sri Aurobindo believe about the nature of education?

Sri Aurobindo believed that education should be a holistic process that nurtures the body, mind, and spirit. He believed that true education is the cultivation of one’s true self and the realization of one’s fullest potential.

3. How did Mahatma Gandhi view education?

Mahatma Gandhi believed that education is not just the acquisition of knowledge but the cultivation of one’s character. He believed that education should be accessible to all, regardless of their socio-economic status, and that it should be a tool for personal growth and social change.
