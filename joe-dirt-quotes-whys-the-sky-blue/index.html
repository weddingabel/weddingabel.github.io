Answering the Target of Joe Dirt Quotes Whys The Sky Blue

In the film, Joe Dirt is asked why the sky is blue. His response, while humorous, is not rooted in reality. The true reason why the sky appears blue is because of a phenomenon known as Rayleigh scattering. This occurs when sunlight enters the Earth’s atmosphere and is scattered by particles in the air. Shorter (blue) wavelengths are scattered more than longer (red) wavelengths, which is why we see more blue light in the sky.
